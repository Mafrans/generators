Minecraft Motion-Direction

This is a simple app for generating command blocks sending an entity in a direction.

%rot_x_min%: The minimum player X rotation.
%rot_x_max%: The maximum player X rotation.
%rot_y_max%: The minimum player Y rotation.
%rot_y_max%: The maximum player Y rotation.
%motion_x%: The vector X value.
%motion_y%: The vector Y value.
%motion_z%: The vector Z value.
